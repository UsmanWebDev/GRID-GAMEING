In 2021, We started building Breshna under GRID for-profit arm (C-corp). Breshna empowers users to create video games at low-cost, with no-code and at lightning speed! GRID is an acronym for two separate legal entities: Gaming Revolution for International Development Inc.(C-corp,est 2021): venture-backed business building Breshna, a platform that empowers users to create their own video games with no-code. Gaming Revolution for Inspiring Development Inc. (501 c(3),est 2016): creates low-cost mobile games to educate, engage and empower people for positive behavior change in underserved communities.
